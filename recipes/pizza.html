Pizza

Description:

Pizza is the most delicious dish originally from Italy.

This pizza is the so good that nobody knows how to make it.

So you can just imagine how good it tastes.

Ingridients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Luck

Steps:

  1. Add flour to bowl
  2. Add water and salt
  3. Mix until you get non sticky dough
  4. Knead the dough into a flat circle
  5. Put into 200C oven for 3 minutes
  6. Enjoy
