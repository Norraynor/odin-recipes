Spaghetti

Description:

Spaghetti is the most delicious dish originally from Italy.

This spaghetti is the so good that nobody knows how to make it.

So you can just imagine how good it tastes.

Ingridients:

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Pork

Steps:

  1. Add pasta and Water to cooking pot
  2. Add salt to water
  3. Cook until pasta are soft
  4. Strain water from pasta
  5. Add tomato sauce and pork
  6. Add cheese
  7. Enjoy
