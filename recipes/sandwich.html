Sandwich

Description:

Sandwiches are universal dishes all over the world.

Everybody loves a sandwich.

Ingridients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Cucumber

Steps:

  1. Slice bread and spread butter
  2. Put cheese on slice of bread
  3. Add sliced ham
  4. Add sliced cucumber
  5. Add sliced egg
  6. Sprinkle salt on top
  7. Enjoy
